Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2025  ·  Applicable to all WaFlow users

WaFlow Technologies Pvt. Ltd. ("WaFlow", "hum") aapki privacy ko seriously leta hai. Yeh policy explain karti hai ki hum kaunsa data collect karte hain, kyun, aur kaise use karte hain.

1. Hum Kya Data Collect Karte Hain

Account Information:

  • Naam, email address, password (encrypted)
  • Business naam, WhatsApp number
  • Billing information (securely processed by payment gateway)

WhatsApp Data:

  • Aapke customers ke phone numbers (leads)
  • Inbound aur outbound message logs
  • Message timestamps aur delivery status
  • Customer names (agar WhatsApp profile mein ho)

Usage Data:

  • Dashboard usage analytics (anonymized)
  • Feature usage patterns (product improvement ke liye)
  • Error logs aur performance metrics

2. Data Ka Use Kyun Karte Hain

  • Service Delivery: WhatsApp automation, lead management, follow-ups
  • Account Management: Login, billing, support
  • Product Improvement: Bugs fix karna, features improve karna
  • Legal Compliance: GST, Indian IT Act requirements
  • Security: Fraud detection, account protection

3. Data Sharing — Kise Dete Hain

Hum aapka data kisi ko sell nahi karte. Sirf in parties ke saath share hota hai:

  • Meta (WhatsApp API): Message delivery ke liye zaruri
  • Firebase (Google): Data storage aur authentication
  • Payment Gateway: Billing processing ke liye
  • Legal Authorities: Sirf court order ya legal requirement par

4. Data Security

  • All data encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Firebase Firestore encryption at rest
  • Passwords bcrypt hashed — never stored in plain text
  • Access tokens encrypted before storing
  • Regular security audits

5. Data Retention

  • Active accounts: Jab tak account active hai
  • Cancelled accounts: 90 days baad delete
  • Message logs: 1 year (Starter), 2 years (Pro), 5 years (Enterprise)
  • Billing records: 7 years (legal requirement)

6. Aapke Rights (DPDP Act 2023)

Indian Digital Personal Data Protection Act ke tahat aapko yeh rights hain:

  • Access: Apna data dekhne ka haq
  • Correction: Galat data theek karane ka haq
  • Deletion: Data delete karane ka haq (account close karne par)
  • Portability: Apna data export karne ka haq
  • Objection: Processing se opt-out karne ka haq

In rights ke liye email karein: privacy@waflow.in

7. Cookies

Hum use karte hain:

  • Essential cookies: Login session maintain karne ke liye
  • Analytics cookies: Product improvement ke liye (anonymized)

Aap browser settings se cookies disable kar sakte hain, lekin kuch features kaam nahi karenge.

8. Third-Party Links

Hamaari website par third-party links ho sakte hain. Unki privacy policies hamaari responsibility nahi hai.

9. Children's Privacy

WaFlow 18 saal se kam age ke users ke liye nahi hai. Agar kisi minor ka data accidentally collect ho gaya ho, privacy@waflow.in par contact karein.

10. Changes to Policy

Material changes ke liye 30 days pehle email notification diya jayega. Continued use = acceptance.
