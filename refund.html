Refund Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2025  ·  Effective immediately

WaFlow mein aapka swagat hai. Hum chahte hain ki aap 100% satisfied rahein. Agar koi problem ho, yeh policy batati hai ki refund kab aur kaise milega.

⚡ Short Summary

  • Trial period: 14-day free trial — no payment needed
  • 7-day money-back: First payment ke 7 din andar full refund
  • After 7 days: Refund available nahi (partial credit possible)
  • Process time: 5-7 business days

1. Free Trial

WaFlow 14-day free trial deta hai Pro plan par. Trial ke dauran:

  • Koi payment required nahi
  • Sab Pro features available
  • Credit card required nahi (trial ke liye)
  • Trial khatam hone par automatically downgrade ho jaata hai

2. 7-Day Money-Back Guarantee

Pehli paid subscription ke 7 din andar agar aap santusht nahi hain, hum full refund denge. Koi sawaal nahi poochha jayega.

Eligible hone ke liye:

  • First payment ke 7 calendar days andar request karni hogi
  • Account already free trial use kar chuka ho aur fir paid ho
  • Ek account par ek baar hi 7-day guarantee milegi
  • Terms of Service violation na ho

3. Refund Eligible Situations

  • ✅ Service 7 din se zyada down rahi (SLA breach)
  • ✅ Double charge / duplicate payment
  • ✅ Technical issue jo hamare end se resolve na ho
  • ✅ 7-day money-back window ke andar request
  • ✅ Billing error (galat amount charge)

4. Refund Eligible NAHI Hain

  • ❌ 7-day window ke baad change of mind
  • ❌ Partial month use karne ke baad cancellation
  • ❌ Meta API limits ya restrictions ki wajah se
  • ❌ WhatsApp Business Policy violation ke karan suspension
  • ❌ Aapne setup nahi kiya / use nahi kiya
  • ❌ Annual plan mid-year cancellation

5. Annual Plan Refund

Annual plans ke liye:

  • First 7 days: Full refund
  • 7-30 days: 75% refund
  • 30-90 days: 50% refund
  • 90 days ke baad: Refund nahi (remaining months credit as account balance)

6. Refund Kaise Request Karein

Step 1: Email karein billing@waflow.in subject: "Refund Request — [Your Email]"

Step 2: Include karein:

  • Registered email address
  • Payment date aur amount
  • Reason for refund
  • Transaction ID (agar available ho)

Step 3: Hum 48 ghante mein respond karenge aur approve hone par 5-7 business days mein refund process karenge.

7. Refund Method

Refund usi payment method mein hoga jisse payment ki gayi thi:

  • Credit/Debit Card: 5-7 business days
  • UPI/Net Banking: 3-5 business days
  • Bank Transfer: 7-10 business days

Processing fees (payment gateway charges) wapas nahi milte.

8. Cancellation vs Refund

Cancellation aur refund alag cheezein hain:

  • Cancellation: Future billing rok deta hai. Current period ka access rehta hai.
  • Refund: Current period ka payment wapas. Account immediately close ho jayega.

9. Disputes

Agar aap refund decision se agree nahi karte, email karein escalation@waflow.in. Hum 5 business days mein senior review karenge.

Chargebacks raise karne se pehle hum se contact karna preferred hai — directly resolve karne ki koshish karenge.
